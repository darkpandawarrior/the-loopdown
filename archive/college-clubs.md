# College Clubs: Why Aren't You In Any?

> The case for joining a society when time, sleep, and parties are all in deficit.

College Clubs:
Why aren’t you in any?


They say that there are 3 things that a college student values due to its deficit ; Time, Sleep and Parties. Why then, must one spend their time and lose their sleep by joining a society, all for the possibility of a party? Let me tell you what exactly you are missing out on.

We student’s generally have the bad tendency to waste our time on things that hold no value to us in the long run. It is no hidden fact that along with decent academics, what every company and post graduate universities value are extracurriculars and soft skills. One isn’t going to gain these vital requirements by binging Netflix or memorizing class notes.
For gaining these important life skills and achievements, one has to consider the pros of college clubs and what participating in it will bring you. Let me explain through my own example.

I started out as an Executive Member of ISTE (Indian Society For Technical Education)- MANIT Chapter. Through my efforts, I have earned the position of Head of Finance. There’s your first gain, recognition. Working in clubs, putting in those long hours and finally being rewarded for it, makes all the efforts put in worth it and the pride that you feel, therapeutic.

Second gain, the networking. You get a chance to interact and connect with like minded peers from other branches, learn the shortcuts and experience from the seniors and finally awaken the leader within you by guiding your juniors. All these are opportunities that wouldn’t be experienced otherwise.

Third gain, the memories. From late night planning for your events, to the celebration parties, Farewells, etc., your college life becomes way more enriched and worthwhile. These are the things you will look back on when you reminisce about your college days.

They say that there are 3 things that a college student values due to its deficit ; Time, Sleep and Parties. Imagine that feeling when you go to sleep after all the time you spent on the events paid off and enjoy your celebratory party. Sounds nice doesn't it?

Join a club now. You will not regret it.
